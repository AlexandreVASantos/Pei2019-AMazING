AMazING (Advanced Mobile wIreless Network playGround)

is an outdoor system developed under a concept by Rui Aguiar, which intended to differentiate itself from the existing ones by increased controllability (for the experimenter) and high reproducibility of the tests, even for mobility events. The platform consists of a free access wireless testbed composed by 24 fixed nodes in a 6x4 grid located at the rooftop of Instituto de Telecomunicações - Aveiro
