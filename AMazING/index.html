AMazING
(Advanced Mobile wIreless Network playGround)

is an leading-edge outdoor wireless test system developed under a concept by Rui Aguiar, which intended to differentiate itself from the other by increased controllability and an high reproducibility of the tests. The platform itself consists in a wireless testbed composed by 24 fixed nodes, in a 6x4 grid, located at the rooftop of Instituto de Telecomunicações - Aveiro
